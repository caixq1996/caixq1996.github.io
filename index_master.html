Biography

Currently I am third year M.Sc. student of Department of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University and a member of LAMDA Group(LAMDA Publications), led by professor Zhi-Hua Zhou.


I got my B.Sc. degree in Aircraft Design and Engineering in June 2018 from Northwestern Polytechnical University. In the same year, I was admitted to study for a M.Sc degree in Nanjing University without entrance examination.

Research Interests

Currently I am focusing on the subfields:
  • Class Incremental Learning: classification problems with emerging new class;
  • Imitation Learning: encouraging the agent to dig out expert policy from demonstrations.
